In 1976, the Heritage Foundation of Oswego surveyed architecturally significant buildings in Oswego County, particularly ones with historic significance. The paper copies of these records have been scanned into this searchable database. We invite you to look at and help us update the properties.

In your search, note that the address of a site may only have a road name or location description, not the specific addresses created later for the 911 system. If you do not find your property, please broaden the search to a whole town or street/road and look at the photos and map.
